## What Are Claude Skills?

Claude Skills (announced October 16, 2025) are self-contained packages that bundle instructions, scripts, and resources that enable Claude AI to automatically handle specialized business tasks. They make Claude **faster, cheaper, and more consistent** for repetitive workflows.

## Building a Business with Claude Skills

### Ideal Company Profile

Your automation company would serve businesses by:
1. **Identifying** high-value repetitive tasks
2. **Developing** custom Claude Skills for those tasks
3. **Integrating** Skills with existing business systems
4. **Managing** ongoing optimization and support

### Value Proposition

**What you offer customers**:
- Faster task completion (70-90% time reduction typical)
- Consistent, high-quality outputs
- Lower operational costs
- Scalability without proportional hiring
- Compliance and audit trail capabilities

### Business Model Options

**1. Skill Development Services**
- Build custom skills for clients
- One-time development fee
- Ongoing maintenance contracts

**2. Managed Automation Platform**
- Monthly SaaS pricing
- Host and manage Skills
- Include Claude API costs
- Value-based pricing tied to savings

**3. Industry-Specific Solutions**
- Pre-built skill packages by industry
- Faster deployment
- Lower cost per customer
- Easier scaling

**4. Hybrid Approach**
- Platform for common tasks
- Custom development for unique needs
- Professional services for implementation

### Target Customers

**Best Initial Targets**:
- Mid-size companies (100-1000 employees)
- High volume of repetitive tasks
- Tech-forward culture
- Clear ROI measurement capability
- Budget for process improvement

**High-Potential Industries**:
1. Financial services (proven results)
2. Professional services (high billable hour value)
3. Healthcare (administrative burden)
4. Legal services (document-heavy)
5. E-commerce (content generation needs)

### Competitive Advantages

**Why Claude Skills vs. Alternatives**:
- More sophisticated than simple automation
- More consistent than general AI
- More flexible than traditional RPA
- Better at knowledge work
- Easier to customize than enterprise software
